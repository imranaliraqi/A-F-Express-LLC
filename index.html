About US

Founded in 2016 A&F Express has been creating a work environment for driver’s comfortability to strive and grow as individuals. This trucking company has been founded by one induvial who loves to strive for his position and is hardworking. With the help of his team, this company was able to be built to what it is now. Teamwork, flexibility, and leadership are the main skills needed in this field of work. Our company has an amazing team that makes everything ready and unique for each induvial to extend his/her background experience and gain knowledge in specific areas of needed training. A&F Express spreads across 48 states in the US. Transports general freight-open board, Amazon contracts from terminal to terminal, and drop-and-hook. We have owner-operator and company drivers.
